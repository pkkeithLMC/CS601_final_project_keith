Music

Music is not just a hobby; it's an integral part of who I am. It's a means of communication and self-expression that continues to enrich my life. I look forward to the ongoing journey and the music yet to be created.

Click on the instrument icons for more information about my journey with each instrument.

Piano

I began my musical journey at the age of 7 with piano lessons, which laid the foundation for my musical exploration.
I have served as the accompanist for many local choirs and instrumental groups.
On an international scale, I served as the principal accompanist for the Gibraltar National Choir, immersing myself in diverse musical traditions. Additionally, I had the privilege of playing the organ at a European cathedral for the Church of England.

Cello

I started playing the cello as an adult.
It's an instrument that I always gravitated to because of it's deep sonorous tone.

Tuba

Tuba is my newest instrument. I played the euphonium in a town band, but the tuba player retired and they needed another player.

Saxophone

I played saxophone all through middle and high school. I played baritone saxophone for a while with a sax quartet, but they all moved away so I sold the sax. I still own a soprano saxphone.

Accordion

I took up accordion when I taught at a public high school. They needed an accordion for the school musical and I needed another excuse to buy an instrument.
